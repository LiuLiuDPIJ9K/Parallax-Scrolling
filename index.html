About Us

Our specialists operate throughout the Oslo and we have earned a reputation as one of the country’s most reliable and trusted industrial cleaning companies, with a range of services from de-greasing hard floors to cleaning ducts and cladding.

We are an all-round service provider in Oslo for Real estate, Organizations and Airbnb management. Lime Light Renhold has been on the market since september 2020 serving multiple private and corporate customers. We started as a small provider and today we have over 30 Norwegian and International clients.
